Let us help you find the right match.

Finding the right therapist is one of the most important and difficult steps in getting started with therapy. Our matching tool uses AI to connect you with the right therapist by looking for correlations between your description of the things you'd like to work on therapy and each FPT therapist's unique background, skill set, and area of expertise. Our matcher will pair you with the best-fit therapist at FPT, but we strongly encourage each client to interview more than one therapist if they have the willingness to invest in it. As therapists, we never want our clients to feel stuck in a relationship that doesn't feel right, so investing a little time up front to pick the right person can go a long way. Our matcher will ask for your name, number, and email. We might follow up with you to make sure you were matched with a good therapist (either at FPT or outside of our practice!), but we never use any of this information for marketing emails or calls, nor do we make public or sell this data at any point. While our matching tool is not foolproof, it might take some of the guesswork out of honing in on the right therapist. If you're in crisis or having a mental health emergency, please tap into the resources below for more immediate support. We hope you have a transformative experience in your next therapy journey and are here to help make that happen in any way we can. Let's roll up our sleeves and get to work!
